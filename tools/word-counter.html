What is a Word Counter Online?

Our free online word count tool is designed to provide you with instant, highly accurate statistics for your written content. Whether you're a copywriter striving for optimal SEO density, a student polishing an academic essay, or an editor reviewing manuscripts, our free word and character counter makes auditing text effortless. By processing everything locally in your web browser, we guarantee 100% privacy and security.
