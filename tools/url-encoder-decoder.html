What is a URL Encoder Decoder Online?

URLs can only contain a specific subset of characters from the standard US-ASCII set. Any characters outside this set (such as spaces, emojis, cyrillic, or standard special characters like ampersands, slashes, and question marks that have programmatic meaning in query parameters) must be converted into a safe format for web browsers to transmit. This conversion process is known as percent-encoding or URL encoding. Our url encoder decoder online is an instant percent encoding tool online designed to convert and decode strings seamlessly in your web browser. Since it operates entirely client-side, your query strings and API keys are 100% private and safe.

How to Encode or Decode URLs Online:

Performing URL conversions with ToolNest is incredibly simple. Here's a brief step-by-step guide:

  • Paste Your String: Copy the URL or parameter text and paste it into the "Input Text or URL" text box.
  • Choose the Operation:
    • Click **Encode** to format full-length URLs safely, keeping routing elements (like `:` and `/`) intact.
    • Click **Decode** to turn encoded characters (like `%20`) back into readable ASCII text.
    • Click **encodeURIComponent** to escape all special characters, ideal when you need to **encode urls for query parameters** safely inside another URL query.
  • Copy Output: Click "Copy Output" to save your result.

Key Benefits of ToolNest's URL percent-encoder:

  • Standardized Escaping: Swap between global `encodeURI` and rigorous `encodeURIComponent` functions for granular query formatting.
  • 100% Private Processing: Your data is parsed entirely in your browser using local JavaScript memory. No data is transmitted to our servers.
  • Cross-Platform Safe: Safely escape spaces, parentheses, quotation marks, hash anchors, and UTF-8 characters across all server platforms.
