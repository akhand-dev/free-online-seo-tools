What Is a Text to Slug Converter?

A text to slug converter transforms any title, heading, or sentence into a clean, URL-safe string called a "slug." Slugs are the human-readable portion of a web address that appears after the domain (e.g. example.com/my-article-title). This url slug generator online free tool automatically lowercases your text, strips diacritical marks from accented characters, removes special symbols, replaces spaces with hyphens, and collapses consecutive hyphens — producing a perfectly formatted permalink in real-time.

URL Slug Best Practices for SEO:

  • Keep Slugs Short: Aim for 3–5 words. Shorter URLs are easier to read, share, and rank.
  • Use Hyphens, Not Underscores: Google treats hyphens as word separators but underscores as joiners.
  • Remove Stop Words: Omit articles like "a," "the," "and," "or" to keep slugs concise.
  • Include Your Primary Keyword: Place your target keyword as early in the slug as possible.
  • Always Use Lowercase: Mixed-case URLs can cause duplicate content issues on case-sensitive servers.
