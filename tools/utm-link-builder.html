What is a UTM Link Builder Online?

A UTM Link Builder online is an indispensable tool for digital marketers, social media managers, and campaign runners. When you share a link to your website, you want to know exactly how many people clicked it, where they came from, and which specific campaign drove them to your site. By appending UTM (Urchin Tracking Module) parameters to your URLs, you can send detailed tracking data directly to Google Analytics 4 (GA4) or other analytics platforms.

How to Generate Campaign URLs

Our tool makes building error-free tracking links simple:

  • Enter Your URL: Start by pasting the full destination link (e.g., `https://www.yoursite.com/sale`).
  • Define the Source: The utm_source tells you *where* the traffic is coming from (e.g., `google`, `facebook`, `newsletter`).
  • Set the Medium: The utm_medium tells you *how* the traffic got there (e.g., `cpc` for paid ads, `social` for organic posts, `email`).
  • Name the Campaign: The utm_campaign groups the efforts together (e.g., `spring_sale_2026`).
  • Optional Tags: Use utm_term for paid search keywords and utm_content to differentiate between links in the same ad or email.
